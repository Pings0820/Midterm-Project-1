School Requirements

College Freshmen

  • Form 138 (report card)
  • 4 copies 1 x 1 colored picture
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character

    • Old Students

  • ID card of the previous semester
  • 4 copies of 1 x 1 colored picture
  • Students clearance from previous semester

    • Transferees

  • Honorable Dismissal (Official Transfer Credential)
  • Certified copy of grades/ credits earned from other school
  • 3 copies of 1 x 1 colored picture
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character

    • Returning/Shifting Students

  • ID card of the last semester enrolled for returning students
  • Application firm from the Registrar's Office
  • Clearance from the Office of Students Affairs
  • Clearance from Cashier's office and settlement of back accounts

    • POLICY OF READMISSION

    A student who withdraws from the College may seek readmission by filing a written request for readmission addressed tothe academic council.

    A student to qualify for readmission must:

  • have no pending disciplinary action
  • secure a clearance from the Finance Director for previous accounts

    • Students for readmission will be on probation for one semester and must maintain a passing grade in all subjects.
